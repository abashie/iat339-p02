Our values

At the heart of everything we do are our company values. We stand by them being the set of behaviours that we expect all our people to embrace to further our ambitions for our business, our students and our communities.

We're proud of what we do - we act sensitively with the highest standards of integrity and social responsibility. We enjoy and benefit from diversity.

We're passionate about students and event hosts - our curiosity and student and host insights drive growth. We care for our brands. We're courageous in pursuing their full potential.

We're innovative, constantly searching for new ideas that drive growth and spinning them across our business. We give ourselves and each other the freedom to succeed because this fosters community spirit.

We will strive to be the best - we are restless, always learning, always improving. We constantly set high standards and then try hard to exceed them.

We deliver results, being the world's largest university event market and student life promoters. Working hard with students and event hosts is at the top of our priority.
